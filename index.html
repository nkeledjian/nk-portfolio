About

Hello World! Web/Software Developer in training here. During my teens, I built computers for friends and family, gaining experience with hardware/software installation, and some basic IT experience. I experimented with Java, 3D modelling for video games, and was big into gaming and creating electronic music. Though still interested in technology, my path took me toward the field of psychology where I took on an interest for cognition.

I pursued studies in psychology but still had a big interest in technology, art, and anything related to logic and cognition. I took a leap of faith within the field and went to a clinical program. Not long after, I opted to leave my role as a therapist-trainee, realizing my true passions lied in cognitive research. Fast foward to the present, post-graduate school, I have come full circle and am diving head first into tech!
